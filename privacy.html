Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

PDF Master: Merge & Split (the "App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or files you process using the App.

1. Data Collection

The App operates entirely offline. All PDF files, documents, and any user input remain strictly on your device. We do not have access to your files, and they are never uploaded to any server or cloud service.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, location, or IP address.

2. Analytics and Crash Reports

To improve the App, we may use Microsoft Store-provided analytics and crash reporting tools. These services may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which features are used, crash logs) that do not contain any personal information or file content. You can opt out of such data collection in your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect data. All processing is done locally using built-in PDF libraries.

4. In-App Purchases

When you purchase a PRO upgrade, the transaction is processed by Microsoft Store. We do not receive your payment details. Microsoft's privacy policy governs that process.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
